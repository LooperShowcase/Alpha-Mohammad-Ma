The History Of Plants Vs Zombies


The beginning

In 2004 George fan Joined popcap studios, and they need ideas for a new game and George fan desgined a tower defense game involving plants and zombies. But then he had an idea that the eneimes should attack the towers,So he started desgining Early concepts. Popcap Saw the potential in his idea so they give him a team to work on the game.

Release

In 2009 Popcap Released plants vs zombies to overwhelming success, It hit most downloaded game in over a month and since it was so popular they had to release versions for xbox/pc/3ds/ and more.


Ownership

After seeing Plants vs zombie's success EA wanted to buy popcap, And on july 12th 2011 EA bough Popcap for $650 million. After buying the company they got to work to create plants vs zombies 2, And after 3 of the original game coming out plants vs zombies 2 came out on july 9th 2013.
